Fred Lorenzo

Designer

Fred has had a passionate interest in design and all things web since he was in school. This passion quickly developed into a love of designing for web, and he was enough lucky to be able to make it into a career. In a short amount of time, he’s had the chance to work with some great brands on some cool projects.

As a designer, he is constantly keeping up with new designs, trends and techniques that the design community has to offer. This means there is always something to learn or something to improve on! Fred is most at home in Photoshop, but also enjoys animating and creating code, having focused recently on pushing the boundaries of what’s possible with email.

Outside of design and Photoshop, Fred is a big film fan, with his second home at the cinema. He is also loves music, playing the drums and was once known as the ultimate LOST fan. He is also a Master of the Rubicks Cube.
