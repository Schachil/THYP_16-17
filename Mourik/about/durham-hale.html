Durham Hale

Technical Director

Durham has served his time at all levels of web development, working his way up to Technical Director. He has a varied knowledge of programming languages and a wealth of problem solving experience.

He pulls together all the little details to keep everything running smoothly at any stage of a project. His experience, thoroughness and attention to detail ensures that projects are always completed to the highest standards with minimum hassle.

Outside of work Durham can be found cycling, running and generally just being outside. He currently holds the Engage course record for the annual 10k Abbey Dash of 41 minutes 19 seconds, making him the envy of the office.
