Tom Scott

Digital Marketing Manager

With pre-university designs on being a famous economist, Tom quickly learned that an inability to grasp basic maths could be a major stumbling block. One changed University course later and he is now a digital marketer quickly gaining experience across different brands and industries. Including one very intense summer working on the Team GB social media team during London 2012.

With an eye for detail and a love of making lists Tom revels in bringing campaigns to life as well as building brands up and helping them reach their full potential. The constantly changing nature of the industry is what keeps him on his toes.

If it’s not work hours, it will probably be some sort of sport you will find him either watching or playing, a lifelong Sheffield Wednesday fan (hence the owl) Tom can be found on most saturday afternoons sat on the Kop at Hillsborough…. or in the pub.
