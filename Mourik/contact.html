From the motorway

When entering the City from the M621, follow the City Centre Loop signs for the BBC until you come across the bus station (we’re directly opposite).

From the train station

Leeds City Station is based on New Station Street which is around a 15 minute walk away (or a 5 minute taxi ride if you’re feeling lazy in a hurry).

Parking

Quarry Hill is our closest car park. It’s pay and display, and costs from £1.80 for an hour. The post code for your sat nav is LS2 1UR.

There’s also an NCP car park which is adjacent to Leeds outdoor market. The post code for your sat nav is LS2 7EA
